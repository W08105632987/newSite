General Questions

MonieKing is a cooperative financial platform that helps Nigerians save, contribute, and access low-interest loans through a tax-efficient structure. We partner with Wema Bank to provide secure savings accounts and offer comprehensive financial services including NIN/BVN enrollment and POS aggregation.

MonieKing was founded in 2022 and has been serving customers for over 7 years with reliable financial services. We began in Bwari, Abuja, and have grown to serve over 2,000 active members across Nigeria.

Yes, MonieKing is officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Nigeria. We operate as a registered cooperative society with full legal compliance.

Savings & Contributions

Our contribution system is simple: You fund your wallet through your Wema Bank account, then contribute any amount from your wallet to your contribution account. Contributions are locked for 12 months from your start date, and at the end of the cycle, you receive your contributions plus annual dividends.

You can start contributing with as little as ‚Ç¶10. There's no fixed amount - you contribute what you can afford, whenever you want during your 12-month cycle. This "Pay As You Like" approach makes saving accessible to everyone.

Contributions are locked for the full 12-month period to maintain the cooperative structure. However, if you need funds urgently, you can apply for a loan at 5% interest, which provides access to money without breaking your savings commitment.

Dividends are shared profits paid to all members who complete their full 12-month contribution cycle. The dividend amount is determined annually by the board based on the cooperative's performance. All qualifying members receive dividends regardless of their contribution amount.

Loans

You can qualify for a loan in two ways: (1) You've contributed an amount equal to or greater than the loan you're requesting, OR (2) You have guarantors (other members) whose combined contributions equal or exceed your loan amount. Just one guarantor is needed if their contribution is more than your loan request.

We charge a flat 5% interest rate on all loans. This minimal rate reflects our cooperative nature - we're here to support members, not exploit them with high interest rates like traditional banks.

The standard loan repayment period is 6 months. If you repay within this period, you only pay the 5% interest. If you default, your guarantors' contributions are used to repay the loan plus the 5% interest.

All members are added to our WhatsApp community where you can connect with other contributors and find guarantors. You can also use guarantors you know personally. This system builds trust and strengthens our cooperative community.

Account & Technical

Contact us with your phone number, email address, and full name. You'll receive a welcome email with download links for the CoopHub app and a default password. After downloading, login, change your password, complete KYC verification, and receive your Wema Bank account number to start using our services.

Your Wallet is like a regular bank account - you can deposit, withdraw, and transfer money freely. Your Contribution Account is where you lock funds for 12 months to earn dividends. Money must be in your wallet first before moving to contributions.

There are no fees for joining MonieKing or funding your wallet. When you transfer money from your wallet to external bank accounts after liquidation, standard bank transfer charges apply. NIN and BVN enrollment services have varying fees - contact us for current pricing.

Currently, CoopHub is available on Google Play Store for Android devices. The iOS version for Apple App Store is coming soon. We'll announce when it's ready through our communication channels.
